# Reference Data Sources

This document lists the external data sources used for Phase 1 (Reference Data Pipeline).
Download and place files in the appropriate directories before running the sampling tools.

## Elevation Data

### MERIT-DEM
- **Description**: Multi-Error-Removed Improved-Terrain DEM. Void-filled 90m global DEM.
- **Resolution**: 3 arc-seconds (~90m at equator)
- **Format**: GeoTIFF tiles (5°x5°)
- **Access**: <https://hydro.iis.u-tokyo.ac.jp/~yamadai/MERIT_DEM/>
- **Citation**: Yamazaki et al. (2017), Geophysical Research Letters. DOI: 10.1002/2017GL072874

## Geomorphometric Data

### Geomorpho90m
- **Description**: Global 90m geomorphometric variables derived from MERIT-DEM. Includes
  pre-computed geomorphon 10-class raster, slope, aspect, TPI, roughness, and more.
- **Resolution**: 3 arc-seconds (~90m at equator)
- **Format**: Cloud-Optimised GeoTIFF
- **Access**: <https://portal.opentopography.org/dataspace/dataset?opentopoID=OTDS.012020.4326.1>
- **Citation**: Amatulli et al. (2020), Scientific Data. DOI: 10.1038/s41597-020-0479-6

## Climate Data

### CHELSA
- **Description**: Climatologies at High Resolution for the Earth's Land Surface Areas.
  Used for MAP calibration and climate-drainage density relationships.
- **Resolution**: 30 arc-seconds (~1km at equator)
- **Format**: NetCDF / GeoTIFF
- **Access**: <https://chelsa-climate.org/>

## Checksums

Checksums are stored in `data/checksums.txt` (generated by `tools/sampler` during Phase 1).
